Qinge Xie

谢秦歌

Hi, I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, fortunately advised by Prof. Frank Li. Currently, I also work with Prof. Haixin Duan in NISL Lab at Tsinghua University.

Before Gatech, I received my bachelor's in computer science in Zhejiang University of Technology, and received M.S. in computer science from Fudan University, advised by Prof. Yang Chen.

I visited Aalto University in the Mobile Cloud Computing Group, advised by Prof. Yu Xiao in 2018, and visited the University of Chicago in Sand Lab, advised by Prof. Ben Y. Zhao and Prof. Heather Zheng in 2019.

(Email: qgxie@gatech.edu)
